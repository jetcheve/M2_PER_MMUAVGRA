(RandomWalk, RandomWaypoint & Pheromone) on JBotSim.

INSTALLATION INSTRUCTIONS :

You just have to create a new Project under eclipse, and then, replace the "src/" 
folder by our, and add the other folder "/lib" and "images".

In order to use the library JBotsim, you have to right clic on your project, 
choose properties, then "Java Build Path", go on the tab "Libraries" and finally 
click on "Add Jars ..." and go to search the jar file "jbotsim.jar" in the 
"/lib" folder.
